Macaroni and Cheese Recipe

An easy and tasty macaroni and cheese meal that will melt your heart with just 25 minute prep time. This recipe will be sure to make you come back for more with its simple and cheap yet delicious result.

Ingredients Needed

  • 1 box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Ground black pepper and salt to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Method

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni until al dente, about 8 minutes.
    2.  
  2. While the macaroni is cooking, start making the roux by melting butter in a sauce pan over medium heat. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth. Slowly pour in milk and stir until the mixture is smooth and bubbling. Be careful to not let the milk burn.
    3.  
  3. Slowly add and stir the Cheddar cheese until smooth and melted. Once the macaroni is finished cooking, drain and stir into cheese sauce until coated.
